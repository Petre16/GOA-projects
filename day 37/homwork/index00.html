TOYOTA

Toyota Motor Corporation is one of the world's leading automobile manufacturers, known for its innovation, reliability, and sustainability. Founded in 1937 in Japan by Kiichiro Toyoda, Toyota has grown into a global brand with a presence in over 170 countries.

One of Toyota's key strengths is its commitment to quality and efficiency. The company pioneered the Toyota Production System (TPS), which emphasizes lean manufacturing and continuous improvement. This approach has made Toyota vehicles some of the most reliable and fuel-efficient in the industry.

Toyota is also a leader in hybrid and electric vehicle technology. The introduction of the Toyota Prius in 1997 revolutionized the automotive market by making hybrid cars mainstream. Today, Toyota continues to develop eco-friendly technologies, including hydrogen fuel cell vehicles like the Toyota Mirai.
